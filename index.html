Our Mission

Our unpretentious craft operation is tucked within the beautiful Madison County Winery located in the picturesque hills and valleys of south central Iowa. And although surrounded by uniquely excellent Iowa wines, our zeal to produce truly unique beers is our sole mission.

We are a true microbrewery and carefully hand craft every beer we brew with meticulous attention to detail. We really like it that way, and we think you will too.

Most importantly -- for both us and you, our customers -- is that we have fun brewing beer. This is a part-time gig for all three of us and we truly enjoy the art, science, and history of brewing and above all, bringing smiles to those who appreciate a meticulously produced pint!

We invite you to come out and share our enthusiasm and tip a few at the Madison County Winery. Additionally, look for our beers at select pubs around the greater Des Moines area.

Cheers! Steve Breman - Steve Becker - Brian Sabus
